mac n. do

is a Chicago based multi-disciplinary artist. They use a combination of special effects makeup and Photoshop editing to write stories of queer myths, deities, and beasts because [redacted] never let them join his Dungeons and Dragons campaign. Their work is the troll under the bridge between the gaping chasm that is often felt between cultural mythos and trans/gender non-conforming identities and is a reminder that the villages built under these bridges have always existed.
